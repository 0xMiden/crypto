Expand description

Lifted STARK configuration.

F and EF are generic parameters rather than associated types so that functions bounded by SC: StarkConfig<F, EF> can refer to them directly. Bounds on F and EF are declared once here and inherited by every user.

Required Associated Types§

type Lmcs: Lmcs<F = F>

Fiat-Shamir challenger.
